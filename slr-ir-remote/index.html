SLR IR Remote

Control your digital camera remotely

SLR IR Remote is an Infrared (IR) Remote app for your HTC One and digital camera.

Make use of the IR Blaster in your HTC One to remotely trigger the shutter on your Canon, Nikon, Sony Alpha, or Pentax digital camera.

Your HTC One's IR Blaster
Not just for your TV anymore.

Your HTC One comes enabled with an IR Blaster located behind the power button. It works great as a universal remote for your TV (seriously - try it!), but now you can extend that use to your digital camera as well!

Always on you
No proprietary remotes or other hardware.

Pro, enthusiast, or amateur: chances are, if your camera is on you, your cell phone is not too far away. And since the IR capability is built right into your HTC One, that means there is nothing else to carry!
